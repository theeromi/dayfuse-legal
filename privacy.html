DayFuse Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

DayFuse ("we", "us", "the app") is a personal task manager. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices. We keep it simple because the app is simple: we collect only what's needed to run your account and your tasks.

Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address you use to sign up, managed through Google Firebase Authentication. Your password is handled by Firebase and is never visible to us.
  • Your task content: the tasks you create — titles, descriptions, due dates and times, priorities, status, tags, and completion state.
  • Voice memos (optional): if you record a voice memo on a task, the audio is saved only on your device. It is not uploaded to our servers or synced across devices. Microphone access is requested solely for this feature.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

How we use your information

  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in.
  • To store your tasks and sync them across your devices.
  • To schedule the on-device reminders you ask for.

Notifications

When you give a task a due time, DayFuse schedules a reminder locally on your device using the operating system. Notification permission is requested only for this purpose and can be turned off at any time in your device settings.

Where your data is stored

Your account and tasks are stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore). Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Google processes this data on our behalf as our service provider; see Google's privacy practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Crash and diagnostics data

To find and fix bugs, DayFuse uses Sentry, a third-party crash-reporting service. When the app crashes or encounters an error, Sentry collects technical diagnostic information — such as your device model, operating system version, app version, and details of the error (including stack traces). This is used only to improve the app's stability. It is not used for advertising or tracking, and we do not attach your name, email, or account identity to it.

Sharing

We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for their own purposes. Data is shared only with the service providers needed to operate the app — Google Firebase (accounts and task storage) and Sentry (crash diagnostics) — and where required by law.

Your choices and rights

  • Delete your account and data: in the app, go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated tasks.
  • Access or correct your data: you can view and edit your tasks at any time in the app.
  • Turn off notifications: in your device settings.

Children

DayFuse is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
